The Men’s Fellowship of this church is committed to becoming and creating disciples of Jesus Christ, both inside and outside of our church. We believe in Men being the best as God intended.

As women, our purpose is to encourage, uplift, inform and enjoy this Christian journey together. The Women’s Fellowship is designed to help women be all they we are purposed/called to be in God’s kingdom.

Our youth programs are distinctively youth-centered. The inhouse and outreach programs are not merely concerned with producing “good youths” but rather spiritually-alive youths; youths who passionately love Christ and serve others.



Our Vision is Spiritual maturity of teenagers and service to the glory of God; To be a united Christian force of teens for faith, witness and service to Jesus Christ, Humanity, Christian maturity and development.

The Children Ministry has a variety of activities specifically designed for children. Our Core Values are Honesty, Integrity, Zeal , Humility, Commitment, Excellence in Christ, Moral uprightness just to mention a few.

Restoring the family to the original intentions of God. Couples’ Fellowship aims to bring couples together as a united Christian force, for fellowship, exchange of ideas concerning matters of faith, witness and service to Jesus Christ and others.



Just as “iron sharpens iron”, the Boys Brigade meet to engage on contemporary and spiritual matters, and to learn from the ancient wisdom of the Gospel of Christ on what it means to live as Christian boys in the modern world.

The Girls’ Brigade most important aim is to help girls to become followers of the Lord Jesus Christ, through reverence, self-control and a sense of responsibility to find true enrichment of life.

Our Zonal Fellowships strive to amplify the Christian goals of our church through zonal meetings, fellowships and spiritual growth by motivating our members to become Godly influences in their homes, neighbourhoods and communities.


The Church for the Deaf is a dedicated ministry that provides a welcoming and spiritually enriching worship experience in Nigerian Sign Language. It empowers Deaf individuals to grow in faith, participate fully in church life, and serve God using their unique gifts and identity. Interpretation and inclusive programs ensure no one is left behind in the body of Christ.



Asides the fellowships, we have various organs/groups where you can serve and fan into flame your God-given gifts and talents.

Church workers are essential to the effective functioning of a church, as they work diligently in various capacities to ensure that the church operates smoothly and efficiently. These committed individuals play vital roles in the church’s day-to-day operations, and their contributions are invaluable.

From ushers who welcome congregants, to sound and media crews who facilitate the audio-visual components of worship services, to pastoral care workers who provide counseling and support, church workers serve in diverse and important roles.

Whether it’s organizing events, maintaining the church property, leading small groups, or providing music ministry, church workers bring their skills, passion, and dedication to serve the church and its community. Their Christ-like attitude and willingness to serve in humble, behind-the-scenes roles make them an integral part of the church’s success.

We are grateful for the countless hours of service that church workers give, selflessly devoting their time, energy, and resources to build God’s kingdom.

We have the: Media Unit, Sanctuary Ushers, Church Band, Church Choir, Sanctuary Cleaners, Traffic Ushers, Football Fellowship.
