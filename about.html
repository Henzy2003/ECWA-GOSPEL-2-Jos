About Us

A Christ-centered family where everyone is welcome, valued, and encouraged to grow in faith and love.

ECWA Gospel 2 Jos (G2J) is a Christ-centered family of believers dedicated to worshiping God, growing in His Word, and serving our community with love. We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God, and we are committed to teaching it in a way that is clear, relevant, and practical to everyday life.

At ECWA Gospel 2 Church, we are more than just a Sunday service — we are a community where everyone is welcomed, valued, and encouraged to grow spiritually. Whether you're exploring faith or seeking a place to belong, you'll find genuine relationships, heartfelt worship, and biblical teaching that speaks to real life.

Join us as we journey together in faith, grow in love, and make a lasting difference in our homes, our city, and the world. Come as you are — there's a place for you at ECWA Gospel 2 Church.

Discover Your Faith Journey with Our Dynamic Church Community

At ECWA Gospel 2 church, we believe that faith is not just a moment—it’s a journey. Whether you’re just beginning to explore Christianity or you’ve been walking with God for years, our community is here to walk alongside you.

Through spirit-filled worship, Bible-centered teachings, uplifting fellowship, and life-changing outreach, we offer a place where you can grow, ask questions, and experience God’s love in a real and personal way.

Join us as we grow together in faith, hope, and purpose. Let’s discover what God has in store for your life—together.
